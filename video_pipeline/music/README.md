# MindCore AI -- Background Music Library

Drop your royalty-free MP3 tracks here.
The pipeline picks one randomly on every cinematic video run.

## How it works
- All `.mp3` files in this folder are picked up automatically
- Music plays at **15% volume** under the voiceover — present but not competing
- Tracks shorter than the video loop seamlessly
- Avatar videos are NOT affected — music is cinematic only
